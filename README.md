# pv-system-model

This software project investigates photovoltaic (PV) cells and their role in a renewable energy system.
It aims to model three aspects of a miniature solar energy system:
     1. How a PV cell behaves under different levels of irradiance, and the resulting I-V and P-V curves,
        modelling a solar cell.
     2. How a PV cell's output can be used to charge a capacitor or battery over time, with a simplified
        charging circuit.
     3. Analyses real-life data and estimates how much energy and power a solar panel can output over one day,
        one week, etc. and in different seasons (i.e. summer vs. winter).
